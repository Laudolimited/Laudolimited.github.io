ABOUT US

Laudo Nigeria Limited is a Shipping, marine,logistics, oil and gas services and solution provider. We are a Nigerian Ports Authority licensed shipping company, agent, and barge operator based in Lagos, Nigeria. We render feeder vessel services, barge services, logistics, and transport support to Shipping lines,oil and gas services and solution provider to West African Ports of Lagos, Cotonou, Lome, and Ghana.

To differentiate ourselves from others, we lay emphasis on technical expertise, customer focus, on-time delivery, competitiveness, and partnerships with world-class companies. Our core values include quality/speed of service, professionalism, and a deep understanding of the industry complexities. Our extensive experience and strong grounding in Nigerian culture allow us to respond quickly to client requests and deliver high-quality products and services cost-effectively and on time, locally, regionally, and internationally.


Our Mission

To bridge the gap in the Shipping,marine,logistics and oil & gas industries by providing operations, maintenance, logistics, engineering and project management services.
